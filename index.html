About Barefoot Beach

Barefoot Beach, located in Hope Town on Elbow Cay in the Abacos, Bahamas, is a newly renovated oceanfront two story beach house, designed for up to 16 guests featuring six bedrooms and four bathrooms. It is on the southern end of Elbow Cay and has its own private beach, a spacious living area with high ceilings, a wraparound porch, and a pool overlooking the ocean.

Golf Cart Rentals

The best way to get around the island is by golf cart! You can easily rent some carts for your stay.

There is ample room for golf cart parking underneath the house. There is golf cart parking available in town and at each restaurant around the island, as well.

There are many rental companies on the island. We recommend Getaway Cart Rentals and Hope Town Cart Rentals.

Boat Rentals/Dock Slip

Barefoot Beach's private boat slip is located at Marnie's Creek, a 2-min ride away from the house. It can fit a boat that has up to an 11' beam. If additional dock slips are needed, you can rent slips for a daily fee at Sunset Exclusive Dockage (242-458-9255).

If you need to rent a boat, we recommend Island Marine and Barefoot Boat Rentals - barefootboatrentals@gmail.com (no association with us).

Need to hire a boat captain? Let us know and we'll be happy to put you in contact with a local boat captain.

Food Shopping & Dining

The island is full of delicious food. Be sure to check out: On Da Beach, Abaco Inn / Firefly, Mackey's / Willie's, Hope Town Inn and Marina, Cap'n Jack's, and Vernon's Grocery and Bakery.

There are three grocery stores on Elbow Cay, but the best place to shop is Maxwell's in Marsh Harbour.

Directions

After landing in Marsh Harbour, you'll need to travel by boat to Elbow Cay. You can do this via private boat or the ferry service.

G & L Ferry and The Ferry at the Crossing are some of the ferry services available.

If needed, the house caretakers, Donnie and Tricia Eldon, will arrange to pick you up from the ferry dock and bring you to the house.
